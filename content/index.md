## Policy (v1)
- 2-week cycles, checkpoint-based, self-certified. Slippage eats your own buffer, never the partner's calendar.
- Buffer weeks: end of Aug / Sep / Oct. Catch-up first, else revision + mocks. No new topics.
- Blocked week (thesis, exams) → **Reading Week mode**: digests, mental math, flashcards only. No accountability.
- Weekly puzzle hour (Akanksh leads) · bi-weekly teaching swap · cross-domain mocks each phase.
- Post-mortem every failed problem (template in `templates/`). Spaced revisits: +2d / +1w / +3w via `next_review` frontmatter.
